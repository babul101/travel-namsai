GOLDEN PAGODA ECO RESORT

4.6/5
4.6 good based on 21 reviews

The Golden Pagoda Eco Resort is spread atop a hillock, Noi-Cheynam just off NH 52 at Tengapani in Namsai district, Arunachal Pradesh, India. It is adjacent to the Golden Pagoda (Kongmu Kham) over an area amidst pristine forests while the Tengapani river flows nearby. The ambience is of serenity,spiritual and lots more only experience can let you know. The buildings of the resort are in almost a semi circular order which lets our guests wake up every morning to the awe inspiring sight of the Golden Pagoda. The resort also has a central garden dotted with fruit and exotic plants.

For Booking and other details visit the Golden Pagoda Eco Resort Official Website: www.goldenpagodaecoresort.com
