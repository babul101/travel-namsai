Nearby places outside Namsai district

  • Parshuram Kund,Lohit


    • Parshuram Kund is situated in the lower reaches to the River Lohit. It is a religious place visited by people from all over the country during the Makar Sankranti for a holy dip. It is believed that taking a dip in the holy water of this lake cleanses one from their sin. As recorded in the Kalika Purana that it was here the sage Parshuram washed away his sin of matricide. Religious people, saints come to this Kund on Makar Sankranti which falls on the 14th January every year.It is recorded that over 70,000 devotees and sages take holy dip its water.

  • Glow Lake,Wakro,Lohit


    • Glow Lake is located in Wakro circle of Lohit district. It is a beautiful spot with background of snow clad mountains and rich flora and fauna in and around the lake. It covers an area of about 8 square kilometers at a height of about 5000 ft. above sea level. The nearest road point is near town village on Chongkham-Wakro road from where one has to start trekking for the lake. Owing to absence of proper road, it becomes an adventure trips to the lake. The villages with a backdrop of hills in the distance, the rivers and its banks, the tea gardens, the vast green fields, the monasteries etc are all sights to behold and the hospitality of the local people and their festival etc have to be experienced to be truly appreciated.

  • Digaru Eco Resort,Digaru,Lohit


    • Digaru Eco Resort is located in Tezu circle of Lohit district.It's a beautiful place with river and surrounding flora & fauna. This place has small hut like place where you could have your food and you if prefer better view to eat then they provide places in the middle of the flowing water where you could have a nice view along with food. It is a place where you can go with family and have a chill time. This place is at a distance of 42.5km from Namsai District.
