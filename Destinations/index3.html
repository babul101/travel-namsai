How to Reach

Flight

The nearest airport is Mohanbari (Dibrugarh) airport situated in Assam at 123kms from Namsai. Cabs can be availed on arrival from Mohanbari to travel to Namsai.

Train

The nearest railway station is the ‘New Tinsukia Junction’ which is situated in Assam and at just 79.7 kms from Namsai. Cabs and buses can be availed from Tinsukia to travel to Namsai.

Bus

Namsai is well connected by road with the neighbouring towns of Assam. Daily day and night bus services are available from Namsai to Tinsukia, Guwahati, Siliguri and Itanagar. Cabs or Taxis can also be availed.

Things to keep in mind before Travelling

  • The Inner Line Permit(ILP) is required for both domestic and foreign tourists to visit Arunachal Pradesh. Domestic visitors can obtain ILP by applying online or by visiting the regional offices of Arunachal Government. ILP on arrival facility is also available in some selected railway stations.
  • Foreign Tourist visiting Arunachal Pradesh shall have to pay USD 50 per head as Royalty to the of Govt of Arunachal Pradesh and Application for PAP are to be applied through local approved tour operators only.
  • Tourist can avail their Inner Line Permit(ILP) online at Arunachal Govt.Official Website.www.arunachalilp.com
